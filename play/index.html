See any wallet as a sphere.

Paste an Ethereum address or ENS name. dustopia will fetch the wallet's NFTs and render them as a 3D sphere you can drag, zoom and explore. No connection, no signing, no minting required.

try one of these

Tip: wallets with hundreds of NFTs take a few seconds to load (the renderer caches the result so a second visit is instant). Big collectors like vitalik.eth are a good demo of how the sphere handles density.
