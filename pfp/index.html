Your wallet, as a PFP.

Drop in any Ethereum address or ENS name and download a 4096×4096 PNG of the sphere on a clean white background. Use it as your Twitter, Telegram or Discord avatar.

Download

First-time builds for unseen wallets take 1–3 minutes (we have to fetch every NFT thumbnail and stitch them into an atlas). Once the sphere stops jittering, pick a format.

MP4 is sized for Twitter (≤ 15 MB). PNG is the still avatar.
